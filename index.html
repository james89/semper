Who We Are

SEMPERIDE, LLC is a veteran-owned car transportation company, serving disabled veterans and private clientele.

Our mission is to surpass client's expectations by providing the utmost highest quality of service in the passenger transportation industry within the Greater NYC Metropolitan area. We are committed to provide you on time and reliable service, various vehicle options including late model hybrids, luxury black cars, mini-vans, and SUVs. Our vehicles are always properly maintained and operated safely by professional and experienced chauffeurs. We feature competitive price rates in exchange for great value for our services. If you need to move people our motto is to always provide a ride with precision and total comfort.

We are happy to provide you with complimentary drinks whether you’re travelling to the airport, or just 5 blocks up the road. Rest assured that we’ll get you there in style, glitz and comfort. We also believe in providing the safest rides around. Our drivers not only went through an intense defense driving course, but they’ve all passed background and drug tests.

Services

Airport Transportation

We service every airport in the NYC Tri-State area. We utilize the most sophisticated and up to date software applications to monitor inbound and outbound flight schedules. Most of our requests are pre-arranged for our drivers to remain with our vehicle at curbside for those who need rapid exit and no luggage service. Conversely and upon request, we can also meet your arriving passenger in the terminal as they exit the secure TSA area of commercial flights. Guests are met with a distinctive sign that can contain our logo and the passenger’s last name. We accompany your passenger to the baggage claim area and load their luggage on a cart to transport to our vehicle. We will also transfer baggage from our vehicle to hotel bellmen or apartment doormen.

Missed Connections?

Our state-of-the-art applications, GPS tracking, and dispatching software, combined with dedicated dispatchers insure no missed connections. Our chauffeurs are in constant communication with our dispatch center. The dispatch staff begins monitoring flight status two hours prior to the expected arrival of any aircraft and keeps the chauffeur updated with information. Our GPS tracking system helps us locate our dispatched vehicle location immediately. Every chauffeur is provided with the passenger’s flight and contact information. A simple toll-free call can put your passenger and our chauffeur together in just a minute or so, whether locally or at any large airport such as LGA, JFK or EWR.

Disabled and Wheel Chair Accessible Vehicles

SempeRide is proud to support our veterans and wounded warriors. We provide non-emergency and specialized transportation services to passengers with disabilities or special accommodations. We feel that these folks are a great disadvantage when it comes to accessibility and expediency of obtaining proper transportation services to all points of transfers. Whether the request is for an important medical appointment, airport transfer or leisure ride, SempeRide is always ready to ride. We feature discrete and specially converted modern luxury mini-vans. These vehicles do not feature unnecessary markings or advertisement that indicates that a passenger in a wheel chair it’s on board. We feel that every passenger should be treated equally, with dignity, without stigmas or advertisement to the public regardless of their physical/medical condition.
